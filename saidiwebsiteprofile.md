## ‚ú® Design Summary

The SAIDI website employs a **modern dark theme** with a professional, tech-forward aesthetic suitable for a cloud computing service provider. The design uses a limited color palette of blacks, whites, and steel blue to create a clean, focused experience. Large, bold typography draws attention to key messages, while generous whitespace ensures readability. The layout is organized into clear sections with consistent styling, making navigation intuitive for users seeking information about GPU cloud services.

**Key Design Principles**:
- Dark mode for reduced eye strain and modern appeal
- High contrast between text and background for accessibility
- Consistent use of primary blue accent color for brand recognition
- Clean, minimalist approach without excessive decoration
- Focus on content hierarchy and readability
